Welcome to Hunger for America. Hunger for America is a non-profit organization with a focus on Children of the ages of Three Months- 16 years old who suffering in rural areas or have families that are unable to provide food.

At Hunger for America, we believe that every child deserves the basic necessity of a nutritious meal. Our mission is to make a meaningful impact on the lives of these young individuals by providing them with the sustenance they need to thrive. Through the generosity of our donors, volunteers, and community partnerships, we work tirelessly to alleviate hunger and ensure that no child goes to bed on an empty stomach.

Join us in our mission to create a hunger-free America for the next generation.
